# An IoT Dog Pen

How cool is the internet of things? It has the oppurtunity to make our lives easier by doing everything from a smart fridge telling us when we're out of milk and sending a notification to our phone to cars emailing us when we need an oil change. I recently spent a weekend playing around with the revolutizary idea of machines connected to the internet and I created a smart pen for my dog, "minion".
